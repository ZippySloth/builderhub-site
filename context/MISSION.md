# MISSION.md — BuildrHub Strategy & Positioning

## The One-Line Pitch
We help boring businesses automate their workflows, understand their data, and stop losing revenue to broken processes.

## The Problem We Solve
Most small and mid-size businesses in boring industries (dental, legal, contracting, logistics, finance) are:
- Losing leads because nobody responds fast enough
- Wasting hours on manual, repetitive tasks that could run automatically
- Flying blind with no visibility into their own numbers
- Paying for tools nobody uses or knows how to configure

They know something is broken. They don't know how to fix it. They don't have time to learn. They need someone to just do it.

## Why Boring Industries
- Not price-sensitive on solutions that show clear ROI
- Almost no competition (everyone chases tech startups)
- Refer like crazy — tight-knit industries
- Same problems repeat across every business in the niche → build once, sell many times

## The Three Tiers

### AUTOMATE — Entry Point
Single, focused n8n automations. One problem, one workflow, one price.
Fast to sell (value is obvious), fast to build (templates), easy to maintain.
**Goal:** Get in the door. Generate recurring revenue. Build trust.

### OPERATE — Growth
Multi-workflow bundles for a specific business type.
Example: "New Patient Engine" for dental = Speed-to-Lead + Reminders + Review Bot.
**Goal:** Deepen the relationship. Increase MRR per client. Reduce churn.

### ANALYZE — Premium
Data intelligence layer. dbt models, custom dashboards, automated reporting.
For clients who want to run their business on real numbers, not gut feel.
**Goal:** Highest LTV clients. Strongest moat (data work is sticky and hard to replace).

## Competitive Positioning
- Generic "AI agencies" → not industry-specific, no real implementation
- Enterprise consultants (Deloitte, Accenture) → way too expensive for our ICP
- Offshore dev shops → communication issues, no strategic layer
- Us → specific, affordable, local-ish, builds + maintains + explains it in plain English

## The Moat
1. Industry-specific automation templates (speed of delivery)
2. dbt models and dashboards (clients can't easily rip these out)
3. Fazio's rare combo: finance brain (M&A, PE, DCF) + technical execution (SQL, dbt, Python, n8n)
4. Trust built through the free audit → hard to replicate at scale

## Revenue Model
- Setup fee (one-time): $800–15,000 depending on tier
- Monthly retainer: $150–3,500 depending on tier
- Goal: 10+ automation clients at avg $400/mo = $4,000+ MRR from automations alone
- Then 2–3 Analyze clients at $2,000/mo = another $4,000–6,000 MRR
- Total target: $8,000–10,000 MRR by month 6

## The Sales Funnel
1. **Top of funnel:** Free 20-min audit. "I'll look at your ops and tell you what's broken."
2. **Conversion:** Audit surfaces real problems → proposal follows naturally
3. **Entry:** AUTOMATE tier, single workflow, low commitment
4. **Upsell:** Add more automations → OPERATE bundle → ANALYZE
5. **Retention:** Monthly maintenance, optimization, results reporting

## Primary Channels (in order of priority)
1. Direct outreach (LinkedIn, email) — targeted, personalized, free audit offer
2. Partner referrals — accountants, web agencies, CFO advisors
3. Local business events / meetups
4. SEO blog content (already running, slow burn)
5. Word of mouth from happy clients (compounding over time)
